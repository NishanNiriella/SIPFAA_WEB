Smart Intelligent Pineapple Farming Assistant Agent

About SIPFAA

Pineapple is a perennial climber that grows well in Sri Lanka's low country wet and intermediate zones. The proposed solution is required to use machine learning technology to diagnose diseases related to pineapple and a knowledge base intelligent chatbot to provide remedies for the diseases. It is required to provide a farmer-to-consumer direct communication platform based on the nearest location including requirements optimization. Further, a product differentiation plan to maximize the profit of farmer by analyzing the trends of market must be included.

This smart intelligent pineapple farming assistant agent mobile application guides the user on four main problems related to the pineapple cultivation. Namely,

  • Difficulty in identification of Diseases and Pest Attacks.
  • Difficulty in identification of the remedies for the diseases.
  • Lack of a Buyer-Seller interaction platform for requirements optimization.
  • Knowledge dissemination gap regarding the product differentiation plan to maximize the profit of farmer according to the trends of the market.

Call To Action

Pineapple Disease/Pest Attack Identification

The main objective of this research study is to implement a common platform as a mobile application to assist farmers in diagnosing diseases and pest attacks with their fingertips.

Knowledge Based Expert Conversational AI

Provide an expert solution for farmers by addressing the time constraint in contacting specialist agricultural authorities for the remedial actions to be taken for pineapple diseases and pest attacks.

Wholesale Buyer-Farmer Interaction Platform

Construct Wholesale Buyer-Farmer interaction platform for requirement optimization using Artificial Intelligent based optimization by considering on nearest buyer or seller location, harvest quantity as well as pineapple quality.

Product/Market Differentiation Plan

Generate product/market differentiation plan with decision supportive approach to maximize the profit of farmer by analyzing the trend of market according to the harvested season, fruit quality, quantity and cost.
